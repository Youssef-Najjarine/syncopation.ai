Patient Fall Prevention Reimagined

Patient falls are a significant concern in hospitals and care centers worldwide, especially in elderly and senior care. According to recent statistics, approximately 700,000 to 1 million patient falls occur in U.S. hospitals each year, resulting in up to 250,000 injuries and 11,000 deaths. With the aging population expected to double by 2060, the need for improved fall solutions becomes even more crucial. Recent methods of monitoring are often invasive and costly, and do not provide real-time assistance.
